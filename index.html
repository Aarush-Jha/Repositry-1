What's New

  • An Inter House Competition “ADVERT MANIAC: AD MAD JINGLE COMPETITION” was organized by Vivekananda House on Thursday, 09.05.2019 for the students of grades III-V in the School Auditorium to bring all the management qualities together in one room. Enthusiastic students made creative and intellectual advertisements which were appreciated by our respected principal ma’am Ms. Vandana Midha who complemented the efforts of the students.






  • The junior students showcased their concern for animals and took a pledge to adopt, rescue and love wildlife forever. The little Goenkans in their beautiful attires and dances enacted, depicting that only when we learn to live in harmony with nature, can we expect nature to behave in the manner that has been there for several hundred years, when humans indulge in affecting nature and its composition through deforestation and animal abuse, mankind will be forced to repent for generations.
