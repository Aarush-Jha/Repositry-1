  • Ad Mad Competion

    An Inter House Competition “ADVERT MANIAC: AD MAD JINGLE COMPETITION” was organized by Vivekananda House on Thursday, 09.05.2019 for the students of grades III-V in the School Auditorium to bring all the management qualities together in one room.   • Le Livre De La Jungle - A tribute to wildlife

    The junior students showcased their concern for animals and took a pledge to adopt, rescue and love wildlife forever. The little Goenkans in their beautiful attires and dances enacted, depicting that only when we learn to live in harmony with nature, can we expect nature to behave in the manner that has been there for several hundred years, when humans indulge in affecting nature and its composition through deforestation and animal abuse, mankind will be forced to repent for generations.












Our Mission

To achieve excellence, G D Goenka Public School offers stimulating environment, set opportunities to broaden horizons and facilitate building of knowledge and skills, in an effortless manner for children and teacher, thereby creating a learning institution. To ensure effortless and meaningful learning, G D Goenka Public School R&D Centre incorporates global strategies, innovative and pedagogical practices and make the education system measurable.


Our Vision

We are a forward thinking school that believes that within each child lies an inherent potential that needs nurturing in a harmonious way. Instilling a habit of learning to learn is the cornerstone of the education we offer. We prepare our children and teachers for a rapidly changing world by equipping them with critical thinking skills, a global perspective and respect for core values.
